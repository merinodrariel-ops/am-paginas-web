# Instagram Story New Website Design

## Goal

Create a premium Instagram Story announcing the launch of the new AM Estetica Dental website.

## Chosen Direction

- Approach: `Lanzamiento premium`
- Objective: announce the new website only
- Tone: elegant, high-end, confident, minimal

## Format

- Vertical story
- Size: `1080 x 1920`
- Duration target: `8-12 seconds`

## Visual Language

- Dark carbon background
- Gold highlights / glow accents
- Manrope + Cormorant style pairing aligned with the website
- Slow, refined motion instead of loud effects
- Clean typography hierarchy

## Narrative Sequence

1. `AM Estética Dental`
2. `Nueva web`
3. `Diseño de sonrisa, carillas, AM Aligners y casos reales`
4. `Ya está online`
5. `www.amesteticadental.com`

## Creative Intent

- Make it feel like a luxury brand launch, not a promo ad.
- Emphasize polish and brand consistency with the website.
- Keep the CTA soft: discovery, not hard sell.

## Motion Style

- Fade and reveal transitions
- Subtle glow movement
- Gentle scale/parallax or masked transitions
- No aggressive kinetic text

## Audio

- Optional ambient / elegant background bed
- Video must also work muted

## Success Criteria

- Feels premium and consistent with the website
- Announces the new website clearly in under 12 seconds
- Leaves the URL readable and memorable
