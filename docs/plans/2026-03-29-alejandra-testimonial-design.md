# Alejandra Testimonial Edit Design

**Objective:** Build one emotionally driven long-form testimonial edit for Alejandra plus three short-form variants for TikTok-style testing.

## Inputs

- Main camera: `/Volumes/Samsung_T5/VIDEOS/TESTIMONIOS/Alejandra Ferrari testimonio/testimonio Aljandra ferrari.MP4`
- External mic: `/Volumes/Samsung_T5/VIDEOS/TESTIMONIOS/Alejandra Ferrari testimonio/audio testimonio alejandra ferrari.WAV`
- Supporting B-roll: files in `/Volumes/Samsung_T5/VIDEOS/TESTIMONIOS/Alejandra Ferrari testimonio/` including `MVI_9791.MP4` through `MVI_9795.MP4` and `Ferrari Alejandra/`

## Editorial Direction

- Prioritize emotion over chronology.
- Build around transformation, confidence, self-image, and lived impact.
- Avoid overt selling language.
- Use smile/macro footage to bridge cuts, cover silence trims, and heighten emotional beats.

## Deliverables

1. One master testimonial edit, approximately 2-3 minutes.
2. Three short variants, approximately 50-65 seconds each.
3. Distinct metadata, hooks, and posting copy for each short variant.

## Recommended Structure

### Master Long Edit

- Hook with strongest emotional line.
- Establish how Alejandra felt before the change.
- Move into the experience and what shifted.
- Land on confidence, emotion, and transformation.
- Use B-roll of smile details and macro shots between major thought units.

### Short Variants

- Version 1: emotional revelation.
- Version 2: transformation and self-esteem.
- Version 3: change in smile and confidence.

## Technical Plan

- Sync external WAV to camera video.
- Reframe from horizontal 16:9 to vertical 9:16.
- Trim silences, hesitations, and weak segments.
- Reorder selected lines if needed for stronger narrative flow.
- Mix audio using external mic as primary source.
- Export one long master and three short variants.
- Apply SEO-friendly filenames and metadata.

## Success Criteria

- Speech sounds clean and focused.
- Framing feels intentional in vertical format.
- Long cut feels emotionally progressive, not repetitive.
- Short cuts each open with a distinct hook.
- All outputs are clearly organized and easy to publish.
